Finesse

Pujan 'FNS' Mehta, born March 19 1992, is a 32-year-old VALORANT esports player from Canada who is currently playing for NRG since May 30, 2024. FNS previously played for OpTic Gaming and their most recent match was against Bilibili Gaming on August 13, 2023. The next tournament that FNS is playing in is the VCT 2024 - Americas Stage 2. FNS total winnings currently stand at $189,700.00. FNS has a current rating of 0.85 from a total 8415 rounds played. Other statistics include 177.9 Average Combat Score (ACS), 117.4 Average Damage Per Round (ADR) and an 70.8% Kill, Assist, Survival & Trading (KAST) percentage. FNS also boasts a first blood success rate of 46% followed by a 20.3% headshot percentage.
